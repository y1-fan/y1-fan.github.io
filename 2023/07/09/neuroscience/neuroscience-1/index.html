认知话题推荐书单

心灵哲学

  1. Philosophy of Mind 3rd edition. Jaegwon Kim.
  2. Philosophy of Mind and Cognition 2nd edition. David Braddon-Mitchell, Frank Jackson.
  3. Philosophy of Mind: Classical and Contemporary Readings 2nd edition. David J. Chalmers. 2021.
  4. The Oxford Handbook of Philosophy of Mind. Ansgar Beckermann, Brian P. McLaughlin, Sven Walter. 2009.
  5. Philosophy of Mind: A Contemporary Introduction 4th edition. John Heil. 2020. [1]

心理学哲学

  1. Philosophy of psychology : a contemporary introduction. José Luis Bermúdez. 2005.
  2. Routledge companion to philosophy of psychology. John Symons, Paco Calvo. 2009.

现象学传统

  1. The Phenomenological Mind. Shaun Gallagher, Dan Zahavi. 2021.
  2. Naturalizing Phenomenology: Issues in Contemporary Phenomenology and Cognitive Science. Jean Petitot, francisco J. Varela, Bernard Pachoud, Jean-Michel Roy.1999.
  3. Handbook of Phenomenology and Cognitive Science. Daniel Schmicking, Shaun Gallagher. 2010.

认知科学

  1. Cognitive Science: An Introduction to the Science of Mind. José Luis Bermúdez. 2020.
  2. Mind: Introduction to Cognitive Science. Paul Thagard. 1996. [2]
  3. Mindware: An introduction to the philosophy of cognitive science. Andy Clark. 2014.

神经科学 / 认知神经科学

  1. Cognition, Brain, and Consciousness: Introduction to Cognitive Neuroscience 2nd edition. Bernard J. Baars, Nicole M. Gage. 2010.
  2. Cognitive Neuroscience: the Biology of the Mind 5th edition. Michael Gazzaniga, Richard B. Ivry, George R. Mangun Ph.D. 2018. [3]
  3. Principles of Neural Science 6th edition. Eric R. Kandel, John D. Koester, Sarah H. Mack, Steven A. Siegelbaum. 2021.
  4. From Neuron to Brain 6th edition, A. Robert Martin, David A. Brown, Mathew E. Diamond, Antonino Cattaneo, Francisco F. De-Miguel. 2020. [4]
  5. Neuroscience: Exploring the Brain 4th edition. Mark F. Bear, Barry W. Connors, Michael A. Paradiso. 2015. [5]

  1. 本书的较早版本有一个中译本，为中国人民大学出版社于 2006 年出版的《当代心灵哲学导论》。 ↩︎

  2. 该书有中译本，为上海辞书出版社于 2012 年出版的《心智：认知科学导论》。 ↩︎

  3. 第三版中文版为中国轻工业出版社 2011 年出版的《认知神经科学：关于心智的生物学》。 ↩︎

  4. 第五版中文版为科学出版社 2014 年出版的《神经生物学：从神经元到脑》。 ↩︎

  5. 第二版中文版为高等教育出版社 2004 年出版的《神经科学：探索脑》。 ↩︎
